# Privacy Policy for FocusFine

**Effective Date:** March 29, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 29, 2026

## 1. Introduction

FocusFine ("we," "us," "our," or "the Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 App Usage Data
- **Package names** of apps you use
- **Time spent** in each app (in minutes)
- **Daily usage patterns** and timestamps
- We do NOT collect app content, messages, or personal data within apps

### 2.2 Payment Information
- **Payment records** (amount, timestamp, duration)
- **Google Play Billing tokens** for purchase verification
- We do NOT store credit card details—Google Play handles payment processing securely

### 2.3 Analytics Data
- **Focus Score** and performance metrics
- **Usage trends** and patterns
- **Device information** (OS version, device model)
- Non-identifying analytics through Google Analytics (optional)

### 2.4 Locally Stored Data
All data is stored locally on your device using encrypted Room Database:
- User-configured app limits
- Daily usage records
- Payment history
- Analytics data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
1. **Monitor app usage** to enforce time limits
2. **Display lock screens** when limits are exceeded
3. **Generate analytics and insights** about your usage patterns
4. **Process payments** through Google Play Billing
5. **Improve the app** and user experience
6. **Provide customer support**

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
- All personal usage data is stored **locally on your device only**
- We use Android Room Database with encryption
- Data is NOT automatically backed up to our servers

### 4.2 Server Communication
- **Payment verification** only: Purchase tokens are sent to our backend for validation with Google Play API
- **Optional analytics**: If enabled, anonymized usage patterns are sent for app improvement
- **No server storage** of identifying personal data

### 4.3 Security Measures
- AES-256 encryption for local database
- HTTPS/TLS for all server communications
- Regular security audits
- No unencrypted transmission of sensitive data

## 5. Data Retention

- **Usage data**: Retained for 30 days on your device; older data is automatically deleted
- **Payment records**: Retained for 1 year (required for tax/legal compliance)
- **Analytics data**: Retained for 90 days (anonymized)
- You can manually delete all data anytime in Settings

## 6. Your Rights

### 6.1 Access and Portability
You can view all your data through the app's "Export Data" feature, which provides:
- Complete usage history in CSV format
- Payment records
- Analytics summary

### 6.2 Deletion
- Delete individual usage records: Available in-app
- Delete all data: Settings → Privacy → Clear All Data
- Upon deletion, data is permanently removed from your device

### 6.3 Opt-Out
- **Usage monitoring**: Disable specific apps in Settings
- **Analytics sharing**: Disable in Settings → Privacy
- **Lock screen enforcement**: Toggle Strict Mode off

## 7. Third-Party Services

### 7.1 Google Play Billing
- Payment processing handled entirely by Google Play
- Subject to [Google Play Terms of Service](https://play.google.com/about/play-terms.html)
- We do NOT store full payment details

### 7.2 Google Analytics (Optional)
- Anonymized app performance data
- No personally identifiable information
- Can be disabled in Settings

### 7.3 Firebase (Optional)
- Crash reporting and error tracking
- Non-personal data only
- Can be disabled in Settings

## 8. Children's Privacy

FocusFine is designed for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents can:
- Review all data collected
- Delete data at any time
- Disable usage monitoring
- Contact us to request complete data deletion

## 9. International Users

If you access FocusFine from outside the United States:
- Your data is stored on your device (no cloud transfer)
- You are subject to this Privacy Policy and applicable local laws
- For EU residents: See Section 10 for GDPR compliance
